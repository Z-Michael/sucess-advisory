Success advisory service

If you want to finish your research work and graduate on time, your thesis advisors are here!! We provide a full advisory service for degree and masters students. Students face problems while they conduct their thesis because of unavailability and lack of enough attention from their school advisors. Some might delay their graduation day or graduate with a minimum score while they have the potential to do more. Our advisory service will solve this stressful part of study by closely working with students in every step of their research work.

SERVICES

Research Topic Selection

Help you to select appropriate and easy research idea by considering your area of interest, discipline and expectations from the school

Concept note preparation

we assist to in the approval process of your research idea by developing a solid and convincing concept note

Proposal Writing

We support the full proposal development in each and every step, in writing of introduction, Literature Review, Methodology (qualitative and quantitative)

Data collection tool and Management

: Help in questionnaire preparation, data cleaning, coding and overall data management.

Quantitative Data Analysis

Plan data analysis based on the proposed objective, conduct descriptive and analytical statistics and present the result using tables and figures

Qualitative Data Analysis

We assist to in the approval process of your research idea by developing a solid and convincing concept note

Discussion

Help you to interpret your results, discuss the theoretical and practical implications of your findings, and the relation to existing literature

PowerPoint

Edit your PowerPoint slides to ensure highlights of your study are presented clearly.

Defense

Help in preparation of proposal and final thesis defense. We help you to get ready for possible question that can be raised at the defense
